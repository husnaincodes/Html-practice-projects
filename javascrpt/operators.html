Operators

Operators are special symbols or keywords in JavaScript that perform specific operations on one or more operands. They are used to manipulate data, perform calculations, and control the flow of a program. JavaScript includes various types of operators, such as arithmetic, assignment, comparison, logical, and more. Understanding how to use operators is essential for writing effective and efficient JavaScript code.
